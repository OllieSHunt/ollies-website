# Virtual Monitors

## So What Are We Trying to Achieve?
So, you want a second monitor.

But there's an issue: they cost money. You know what doesn't cost money? That old laptop that's been sitting in a draw for the past few years gathering dust. The laptop may be old and obsolete, but its display works just fine.

So how can we repurpose this ancient piece of tech into a functioning second monitor?

Well unfortunately, its a bit more complicated than just plugging in a HDMI cable.
